Death Eaters Among Us

A Harry Potter Themed Mafia Game

12 Players

Cut out the role cards and shuffle them.
Distribute one card to each player face-down.
Players should keep their roles secret!

Death Eater

EVIL TEAM

"The Dark Mark burns on your arm..."

Your Role

You are a Death Eater serving the Dark Lord. Your mission is to eliminate Order members while avoiding detection.

Instructions

  • You are a Death Eater. Keep this secret!
  • Your Dark Mark burns, revealing other Death Eaters to you.
  • Each night, work with fellow Death Eaters to eliminate an Order member.
  • During the day, blend in and avoid suspicion.
  • Win by equaling or outnumbering the Order.

Dumbledore

GOOD TEAM

"Your wisdom allows you to sense dark magic..."

Your Role

You are Albus Dumbledore. Use your wisdom to sense the presence of dark magic.

Special Ability: Sense Dark Magic

Point to one player during the night. The Game Master will reveal if they serve the Dark Lord or the Order.

Instructions

  • You are Dumbledore. Keep this secret!
  • Each night, you can sense if someone has the Dark Mark.
  • Use your wisdom carefully without revealing yourself.
  • Win by eliminating all Death Eaters.

Madam Pomfrey

GOOD TEAM

"Your healing magic can save lives..."

Your Role

You are Madam Pomfrey, the Hogwarts healer. Use your healing magic to save Order members from Death Eater attacks.

Special Ability: Healing Magic

Use healing magic on one player during the night to protect them from Death Eaters. You can protect yourself. Important: You cannot protect the same player two nights in a row.

Instructions

  • You are Madam Pomfrey. Keep this secret!
  • Each night, use your healing magic on one player.
  • You cannot protect the same player twice in a row.
  • Win by eliminating all Death Eaters.

Order Member

GOOD TEAM

"You fight for the light against the darkness..."

Your Role

You are a member of the Order of the Phoenix. Use your voice and vote to identify Death Eaters.

Instructions

  • You are an Order Member.
  • You have no special abilities.
  • Use logic and discussion to find the Death Eaters.
  • Vote to send suspected Death Eaters to Azkaban.
  • Win by eliminating all Death Eaters.
